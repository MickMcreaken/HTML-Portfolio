Mickey's Top 3 Movies

The best of Science Fiction

1. Avatar

The special effects were fantastic, the world was immersive, and it was the first movie I was ever able to enjoy with the new 3d technology.

2. Hitch Hiker's Guide to the Galaxy

Based on one of my favorite books of all time, it is the most ironically entertaining movies I have ever seen

3. Idiocracy

Well on its way to being a social documentary, this was just a feel good movie.
